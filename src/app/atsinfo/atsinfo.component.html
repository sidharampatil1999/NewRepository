What is an Applicant Tracking System (ATS)?

An ATS, or Applicant Tracking System, is a software tool designed to automate and enhance recruitment. It is a central hub for managing job postings, storing applicant data, and streamlining the hiring process. ATS plays a key role in helping recruiters sift through large volumes of applications. It uses advanced algorithms to scan resumes and applications for specific keywords and criteria, ensuring that only the most relevant candidates are shortlisted for further review. This not only saves time but also helps in maintaining a high standard of candidate selection.
